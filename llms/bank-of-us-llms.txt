# Bank of us

> Bank of us (B&E Ltd, brand "BNE LTD") is Tasmania's only customer-owned mutual bank, a regulated authorised deposit-taking institution (ADI) headquartered in Launceston. As an active Consumer Data Right (CDR) data holder it exposes a public, unauthenticated Product Reference Data (PRD) API conforming to the DSB Consumer Data Standards, alongside the accredited-data-recipient consumer-data-sharing channels required of every Australian bank.

## APIs

- [Bank of us CDR Product Reference Data API](https://www.bankofus.com.au/open-banking): Public, unauthenticated CDR Product Reference Data (PRD) API exposing Bank of us's publicly available banking products (home loans, personal/business banking, savings, term deposits) via GET /banking/products and GET /banking/products/{productId}. Live base: https://api.bankofus.com.au/OpenBanking/cds-au/v1 (HTTP 200, x-v 4, 27 products).
